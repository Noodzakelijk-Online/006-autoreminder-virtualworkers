**Design Movement:** "Neo-Brutalist Productivity"
**Core Principles:**
1. **High Contrast & Clarity:** Bold borders, distinct sections, no ambiguity.
2. **Data-First:** Information density is high but structured.
3. **Action-Oriented:** Clear states (todo, doing, done).
4. **Raw & Honest:** Shows workload realistically without hiding complexity.

**Color Philosophy:**
- **Background:** Off-white (#F5F5F0) or light grey (#E5E5E5)
- **Accents:**
  - **Critical:** Sharp Red (#FF3B30)
  - **Urgent:** Safety Orange (#FF9500)
  - **Normal:** Deep Blue (#007AFF)
  - **Done:** Success Green (#34C759)
- **Text:** Stark Black (#000000) for maximum readability

**Layout Paradigm:**
- **Split Screen:** Left sidebar for navigation/stats, right main area for timeline.
- **Card-Based:** Each task is a distinct card with hard edges and drop shadows.
- **Timeline:** Vertical timeline for daily view, horizontal for weekly.

**Signature Elements:**
- **Thick Borders:** 2px-3px black borders on cards.
- **Monospace Fonts:** For time and data (e.g., `08:00 - 09:30`).
- **Status Tags:** Pill-shaped tags with bold text.

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- **Hover Effects:** Cards lift up (shadow grows) on hover.
- **Click:** Expands card to show details (attachments, Dutch flags).
- **Check:** Satisfying "stamp" animation when completing tasks.

**Animation:**
- **Snappy:** Fast transitions (150ms), no easing.
- **Slide-in:** New tasks slide in from right.

**Typography System:**
- **Headings:** Bold, sans-serif (e.g., 'Space Grotesk' or 'Archivo').
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (e.g., 'Inter' or 'Public Sans').
- **Data:** Monospace (e.g., 'JetBrains Mono' or 'Roboto Mono').

## Selected Approach: "Soft Minimalist Flow"

**Reasoning:**
Joyce needs a dashboard that reduces stress and helps her focus. The "Soft Minimalist Flow" approach prioritizes calmness and clarity, which is essential for managing a high workload. The gentle colors and fluid interactions will make the dashboard feel like a helpful assistant rather than a demanding taskmaster.

**Implementation Details:**
- **Font:** 'Nunito' for headings, 'Lato' for body.
- **Colors:** Warm white background, soft pastel accents for priorities.
- **Components:** Rounded cards, floating stats panel, vertical timeline with connecting line.
- **Visuals:** Abstract, calming background patterns (generated).
